- **IT (2 hrs):** Focus entirely on **C++, Java, or Python basics**. Don't worry about "Data Structures" yet. Master Loops, If-Else, Functions, and Arrays.
    
- **Quant (1 hr):** Focus on **Speed Math** (Percentages, Fractions, Tables up to 30, Squares/Cubes). This is the engine for all competitive exams.
    
- **Reasoning (1 hr):** Master **Syllogisms and Inequalities**. These are "easy marks" you shouldn't miss.
    
- **English/GA (1.5 hrs):** Read one financial editorial (like _LiveMint_ or _Economic Times_) daily and write a 3-sentence summary.
