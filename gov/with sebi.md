## **SEBI Grade A (IT)**

- **Dates:** Notification ~June 2026; Exams ~October–November 2026.
    
- **Posts:** ~25–35.
    
- **Salary:** ~₹1,55,000/month.
    
- **Syllabus:**
    - **Phase 1:** Aptitude (Qualifying) + IT Basics.
    - **Phase 2:** Advanced IT (Java, SQL, Networking, OS) + Descriptive English.

## **SBI SO (IT Specialist)**

- **Dates:** Multiple cycles throughout 2026.
    
- **Posts:** 50–100+.
    
- **Salary:** ₹70,000 – ₹1,80,000 (varies by scale).
    
- **Syllabus:** Often a single **Technical Test** (Software Dev, Infra, or Security focus) followed by an Interview.

## **PFRDA Grade A (IT)**

- **Dates:** Notification ~Sept 2026.
    
- **Posts:** 2–5.
    
- **Salary:** ~₹1,40,000/month.
    
- **Syllabus:** Identical to SEBI (Phase 1 Aptitude + Phase 2 Tech/English).


## **IFSCA Grade A (IT)**

- **Dates:** Notification ~March/April 2026; Exams ~May/June 2026.
    
- **Posts:** 2–5.
    
- **Salary:** ~₹1,45,000+/month.
    
- **Syllabus:** Identical to SEBI (Phase 1 Aptitude + Phase 2 Tech/English).


## **IBPS SO (IT Officer)**

- **Dates:** Prelims: Aug 29, 2026; Mains: Nov 1, 2026.
    
- **Posts:** 100–300+.
    
- **Salary:** ~₹60,000 – ₹70,000/month.
    
- **Syllabus:**
    
    - **Prelims:** Quant, Reasoning, English (Qualifying).
        
    - **Mains:** 100% Professional Knowledge (CS Core: DBMS, Networking, OS, DS).
