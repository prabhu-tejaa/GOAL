## 1. Banking & Financial Regulatory Bodies

These are high-salary "Officer" roles. Most have an age limit of 30 for General, but **33 for BC-C**.

- **IBPS SO - IT Officer (Scale-I):** Primary technical role in public banks.
    
- **SBI Probationary Officer (PO):** High-prestige general management.
    
- **IBPS PO:** General management across multiple public banks.
    
- **RBI Grade B (Generalist & IT Manager):** Top-tier central bank roles.
    
- **SEBI Grade A Officer (IT):** Markets regulator technical role.
    
- **NABARD Grade A (IT & General):** Rural development bank management.
    
- **SIDBI Grade A (IT Officer):** Small industries development management.
    
- **NHB Assistant Manager (IT):** National Housing Bank.
    
- **IDBI Bank Specialist Officer (IT & MIS):** Private-sector style gov-linked bank.
    
- **ECGC PO (IT):** Export credit insurance management.
    

---

## 2. Insurance Sector Officers

- **NIACL IT Officer (Scale I):** New India Assurance technical role.
    
- **LIC AAO (Generalist & IT Specialist):** Life insurance administrative/technical officer.
    
- **GIC Re (IT Officer):** General Insurance Corporation.
    
- **AIC IT Management Trainee:** Agriculture Insurance Company.
    

---

## 3. Central Civil Services & Administration

- **UPSC Civil Services (IAS, IPS, IRS, etc.):** Elite administrative services (Limit: 35 for BC-C).
    
- **UPSC EPFO (APFC & EO/AO):** Top-tier social security and enforcement roles (Limit: 38 for BC-C).
    
- **SSC Combined Graduate Level (CGL):** Income Tax, Excise, and ASO roles.
    
- **UPSC Assistant Registrar:** High-level admin for Central Universities/IITs (Limit: 43 for BC-C).
    
- **NITI Aayog (Young Professional / Associate):** Policy and data analysis.
    
- **SSC Selection Posts:** Specialized niche roles in various Central ministries.
    

---

## 4. Scientific, Technical & Research Organizations

These specifically require your **MCA/B.Sc (CS)** background.

- **Scientist 'B' (NIC / NIELIT / BIS):** Core government software and standards roles.
    
- **BARC Scientific Officer (CS):** Bhabha Atomic Research Centre.
    
- **Scientific/Technical Assistant (A):** Support roles in ISRO, DRDO, or NIC.
    
- **C-DAC (Project Engineer / Knowledge Associate):** High-end R&D in computing.
    
- **NTRO (National Technical Research Organisation):** Specialized technical intelligence.
    
- **ERNET India (Programmer/Systems Analyst):** Ministry of Electronics.
    

---

## 5. Security, Intelligence & Law Enforcement

- **Intelligence Bureau (IB) - ACIO Grade II (Technical):** Technical intelligence gathering.
    
- **IB Security Assistant (Technical):** Technical security maintenance.
    
- **CBI Sub-Inspector (Technical):** Cybercrime and technical investigation.
    

---

## 6. Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs)

Most of these offer excellent housing and medical benefits.

- **BPCL Junior Executive:** Oil and gas systems/management.
    
- **Programming Officer (E-1 Level):** Typically ONGC or similar energy PSUs.
    
- **BHEL / SAIL / NTPC / BEL:** Various IT and Executive Officer roles.
    
- **AAI Junior Executive (IT):** Airports Authority of India.
    
- **Ircon International (Railways):** Infrastructure management.
    
- **HLL Lifecare IT Officer:** Healthcare PSU systems.
    

---

## 7. Forestry & Wildlife Roles

- **Indian Forest Service (IFS) - UPSC:** Top-tier forest administration.
    
- **Forest Range Officer (FRO):** Field-level forest management (State PSC).
    
- **OSSSC / State Forester:** Field executive roles.
    
- **WII Administrative Assistant:** Wildlife Institute of India logistics.
    
- **ICFRE Technical Assistant:** Forestry research support.

## 8. Education, Transport & Others

- **UGC NET / Assistant Professor:** Higher education teaching (No age limit for Prof).
    
- **KVS / NVS PGT (Computer Science):** School-level teaching (Limit: 43 for BC-C).
    
- **RRB Junior Engineer & SSE (IT):** Railway technical wings (Limit: 39 for this cycle).
    
- **Metro Rail (Station Controller / JE IT):** Urban transport management.
    
- **FCI Manager & Assistant Grade III:** Food security and logistics.
    
- **State PSC (Deputy Collector, BDO, DSP):** State-level power roles.
    
- **UPSSSC Assistant Statistical Officer (ASO):** Data and statistics.
    
- **UIDAI / TRAI / FSSAI Advisor & Programmer:** Various statutory bodies.
