 ###  1. Specialist Officer (SO) - IT Officer (Scale-I)

This is the primary entry point for MCA graduates into the public sector banking system.

- **Organization:** IBPS (Institute of Banking Personnel Selection)
    
- **Age Limit (BC-C):** Up to **33 years** (30 + 3 years relaxation).
    
- **Notification Timeline:** Expected **June 2026**.
    
- **Exam Window:** Preliminary exams are scheduled for **August 2026**, with Mains in **November 2026**.
    

### 2. SEBI Grade A Officer (Information Technology)

A prestigious regulatory role focusing on the technical systems of the Indian securities market.

- **Organization:** Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI)
    
- **Age Limit (BC-C):** Up to **33 years**.
    
- **Notification Timeline:** Expected **October/November 2026** (based on current 2025-26 cycle trends).
    
- **Salary:** High pay scale (approx. ₹1.4 Lakh+ monthly).
    

### 3. NABARD Grade A - Assistant Manager (IT)

Technical management within the National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development.

- **Organization:** NABARD
    
- **Age Limit (BC-C):** Up to **33 years**.
    
- **Notification Timeline:** Expected **September 2026**.
    
- **Education Requirement:** 55% aggregate in MCA for reserved categories.
    

### 4. Scientist 'B' (Computer Science)

A pure technical/research role involved in developing government software and infrastructure.

- **Organization:** NIC (National Informatics Centre) / NIELIT
    
- **Age Limit (BC-C):** Up to **33–35 years** (varies by specific notification).
    
- **Notification Timeline:** Periodic; last major cycle notification was **April 2026**. Check the NIELIT portal for late-year or 2027-early announcements.
    

### 5. Programming Officer (E-1 Level)

A technical executive role within India’s largest oil and gas producer.

- **Organization:** ONGC (Oil and Natural Gas Corporation)
    
- **Age Limit (BC-C):** Up to **33 years**.
    
- **Selection Basis:** Typically requires a valid **GATE score** in Computer Science, followed by an interview.
    
- **Timeline:** Recruitment usually opens after GATE results (post-March 2026).
    

### 6. IT Officer (Scale II / Scale I) in Regional Rural Banks

- **Organization:** IBPS RRB
    
- **Age Limit (BC-C):** Up to **33 years** for Officer Scale I; Scale II often requires 1-2 years of industry experience.
    
- **Notification Timeline:** Expected **June 2026**.
    

### 7. Scientific Assistant / Technical Assistant (A)

- **Organization:** NIC / ISRO / DRDO
    
- **Age Limit (BC-C):** Up to **33 years**.
    
- **Role:** Supporting scientific projects and maintaining IT infrastructure.
    
- **Timeline:** Varies; notifications are individual to each organization’s requirement cycle.
