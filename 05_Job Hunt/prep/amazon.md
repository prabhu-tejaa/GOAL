### Phase 1: The Online Assessment (OA)

The OA is proctored and typically lasts between **90 to 120 minutes**. In 2026, Amazon has added interactive and AI-related components.

- **Part 1: Coding Assessment (70 Minutes)**
    
    - **Format:** 2 DSA problems.
        
    - **Goal:** Solve both with optimal Time Complexity (O(N) or O(NlogN)).
        
    - **What to Prepare:**
        
        - **Arrays & Strings:** Two-pointers, Sliding Window, and Hashing.
            
        - **Trees & Graphs:** BFS/DFS traversal is very common.
            
        - **New for 2026:** "Optimization" questions where you might need to find a maximum or minimum median of a subsequence.
            
    - **Pro-Tip:** Aim to finish Question 1 in **25 minutes** to leave 45 minutes for Question 2, which is significantly harder.
        
- **Part 2: Work Simulation (25-60 Minutes)**
    
    - **Format:** A "Day in the Life" module where you handle emails, instant messages, and bugs.
        
    - **What to Prepare:**
        
        - **Decision Making:** You will be asked to rank or select the best response to a teammate or customer.
            
        - **AI Coworker (2026 update):** You may have to chat with an AI assistant to make ethical or technical trade-off decisions.
            
        - **Focus:** Choose answers that reflect **Customer Obsession** and **Ownership**.
            
- **Part 3: Work Style Assessment (15 Minutes)**
    
    - **Format:** A personality survey with "Strongly Agree" to "Strongly Disagree" options.
        
    - **What to Prepare:**
        
        - **Consistency:** The system flags contradictory answers.
            
        - **Amazon DNA:** Read the **16 Leadership Principles**. For example, if a question asks if you'd rather wait for a perfect plan or move forward with 70% data, choose "move forward" to show **Bias for Action**.
